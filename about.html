About the movie
My team members and I worked on this film, and made it from start to finish in only 30 minutes. We wanted to depict the stress of course registration in an exagerrated and humorous way. We used sound effects and music to show the time pressure and the implication that your course selection can have on your future. We really enjoyed making the film, from acting in it, to editing the clips and adding sounds. We hope you enjoyed watching the film!
